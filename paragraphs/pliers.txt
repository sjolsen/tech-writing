The familiar /slip joint pliers/ are named for the two-position pivot.
It provides both normal and wide jaw openings.
Broad-jawed /linemart's pliers/ have side cutters.
This equips them for heavy-duty wire cutting and splicing.
/Channel-type pliers/ with multiposition pivots adjust.
Jaw openings are up to 2 inches.
They grip any shape.
/Long-nosed pliers/ are used to shape wire.
They shape thin metal.
They cut as well.
/Diagonal-cutting pliers/ have no gripping jaws.
They cut only.
Cutting only are /end cutting nippers/.
They snip wire, small nails, and brads.
