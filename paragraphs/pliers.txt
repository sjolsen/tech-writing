The familiar /slip joint pliers/ are named for the two-position pivot which provides both normal and wide jaw openings.
Broad-jawed /linemart's pliers/ have side cutters which equips them for heavy-duty wire cutting and splicing.
/Channel-type pliers/ with multiposition pivots can be adjusted to a jaw opening of up to two inches and grip any shape.
/Long-nosed pliers/ are used to shape wire, shape thin metal, and cut.
/Diagonal-cutting pliers/ have no gripping jaws and only cut.
/End cutting nippers/ are also limited to cutting, able to snip wire, small nails, and brads.
