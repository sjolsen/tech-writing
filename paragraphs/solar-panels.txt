The solar panels will be installed on each end of the center.
They will collect enough solar energy to power add the space stations systems.
There are two materials that are being considered for use in constructing the main superstructure.
The first is carbon fiber.
Carbon fiber is a relatively new material.
It is composed of carbon fibers bonded together by epoxy resin.
It has many advantages to other materials.
Carbon fiber is up to as three times as strong as steel.
Steel weighs twice as much as carbon fiber.
