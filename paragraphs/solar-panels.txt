There are two materials that are being considered for use in constructing the main superstructure.
The first, carbon fiber, is a relatively new material composed of carbon fibers bonded together by epoxy resin.
It has many advantages to other materials; for example, carbon fiber is up to as three times as strong as the second material, steel, which weighs twice as much as carbon fiber.
